This Site Is Not Written By AI

2024-10-18 | 2024-10-19 | 151 words | 1 min
#updates #notbyai #linkbuttons

A new link button has been added to this website. As of the time writing this you may notice a badge at the site footer that looks like this:

This badge is provided by Not by AI, an organization focused on promoting content originality. I decided to add this after seeing it on this guy’s site. This should indicate to you that, most likely, the content written for this site is not AI generated slop made for search engine optimization and maximum eyeball retention… it’s only human generated slop. On a side note, and perhaps it is my fault for contributing to this, it is a bit dystopian that we’ve gotten to the point that people find it necessary to distinguish that their content is original, either as a point of pride or because in a lot of cases non-ai generated material has become impossible to recognize in a vacuum. sigh.
